Destinations We Travel Most
Click any destination to explore flights, hotels, and travel tips.

Punakha

Kuruthang

Punakha is one of the most beautiful and historically important valleys in Bhutan, known for its warm climate, fertile lands, and peaceful riverside scenery. Located at the confluence of the Pho Chhu (male river) and Mo Chhu (female river), Punakha has a unique natural beauty that feels calm and deeply refreshing.

Paro Vally

khangkhu

Paro is one of the most beautiful and culturally rich valleys in Bhutan, known for its peaceful atmosphere, stunning landscapes, and deep spiritual heritage. Surrounded by green mountains, rice fields, and traditional Bhutanese homes, Paro feels calm and timeless compared to busy modern cities.

Wangdue Phodrang

Bajo

Wangdue Phodrang Dzong is one of the most important and historic fortresses in Bhutan, standing proudly in the central region of the country

Thimphu

Buddha Point

Buddha Point (Kuensel Phodrang) is one of the most iconic and peaceful landmarks in Bhutan, located just above Thimphu city

Thimphu

viewpoint

Thimphu View Point is one of the most peaceful and scenic spots overlooking the capital city of Bhutan.
